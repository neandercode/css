This has been my professional field since the dawn of my 20s. With over a decade in the field, I have seen it all. The process of getting a floor from dirty to clean has never gotten old. One day, I hope to own a small business to do it in my free time.

Decent Drummer

My first passion I came across when I was 14 years old. I still remember the christmas my Mom took my brother and I to Guitar Center to pick out our very first instruments. Mine was a Gregg's Percussion. A simple 5 piece with a high hat and crash. My brother and I would jam 8 days a week trying to recreate sounds of Killswitch Engage or any other metalcore sound (yes, its still a great genre).

They taught me the importance of empathy and how to really communicate with animals without the need for a voice.
